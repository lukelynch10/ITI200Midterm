Monty Python and The Holy Compiler

Sir Arthur was a brave and noble knight who had a passion for programming. He had learned many languages, such as Python, Java, and C++, but he was always curious about the legendary language HolyC, which was said to be the most powerful and elegant language ever created. However, HolyC was also shrouded in mystery, as no one had ever seen or used it. The only way to access HolyC was to find and use the holy compiler, a mythical tool that could translate the HolyC source code.

Monty Python and The Holy Compiler is a text based RPG game where you must complete your journey within 10 steps. The game includes features such as:

  • Combat
  • Health
  • Tough Decisions
  • Interesting Encounters

Our game will allow you to be able to compete with friends to see who can get the highest score. We will have a database to store things like:

  • Player name
  • High Score
  • Scenarios Encountered
